# 13 -- Property Records: 814 and 819 Malta Lane, Silver Spring, MD

**Date: 2026-03-19**
**Central question: Who owned the two key properties, and what does the ownership history reveal?**

## Executive Summary

Both 814 and 819 Malta Lane are in the Kemp Mill neighborhood of Silver Spring (ZIP 20901), built in 1959 as part of the same postwar suburban development. They sit on opposite sides of the same street -- even numbers on one side, odd on the other -- placing them approximately 15-30 meters apart. Akwei's residence at 819 and the Kinnecome residence at 814 are independently confirmed through multiple sources.

### Ownership Gap

No property deed has been found online explicitly linking "Kinnecome" to 814 Malta Lane. The current owner is Robynne A. Williams. Maryland SDAT and Montgomery County land records require interactive database access for historical deed searches. Possible explanations:
- Kinnecomes may have been renters, not owners
- Property may have changed hands through an intermediary
- Historical deed records may not be digitized in the online portal
- The children may have sold after Robert's death

### The 814 vs. Riverdale Question

Robert's 1994 obituary lists his address as **Riverdale, MD** -- not Malta Lane. His children were "all of Silver Springs." This creates a question:

**Possibility A:** Robert lived at 814 Malta Lane with his children but moved to Riverdale after "retiring to enter private practice." The children remained at 814. This would mean the active operational presence at 814 preceded Robert's move.

**Possibility B:** 814 Malta Lane was always the children's residence. Robert lived elsewhere (Riverdale) and the children conducted the alleged surveillance operations independently or under his remote direction.

**Possibility C:** Robert was at 814 Malta Lane during the 1980-1992 period (matching Akwei's complaint timeline) and moved to Riverdale after the lawsuit was dismissed (post-March 1992). He died at the Riverdale address in September 1994.

Possibility C is most consistent with the timeline: Akwei filed in 1992 naming Robert as a resident of 814 Malta Lane, and Robert's obituary two years later gives a different address.

## 4. The 2007 Sale

Claire M. Akwei sold 819 Malta Lane in 2007 for $407,000. This is 15 years after the lawsuit and 13 years after Robert's death. Possible reasons:
- Claire was aging (John's mother -- likely in her 70s or 80s by 2007)
- John had been working steadily since 1994 and may have moved elsewhere
- The property no longer served as the family home
- No ongoing reason to remain on Malta Lane after the threat had ended

The timing does NOT correlate with any known Kinnecome event. It appears to be a normal life-stage property sale.
