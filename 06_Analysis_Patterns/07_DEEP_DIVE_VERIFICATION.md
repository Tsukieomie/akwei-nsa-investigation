1. **Named individuals are real** -- at least 2-3 of 5 Kinnecome family members confirmed in the Maryland corridor

3. **The judge had a conflict** -- former CIA General Counsel, 6.7% random assignment probability
4. **Independent corroboration exists** -- Tice confirmed domestic electronic warfare at NSA

6. **Geographic proximity** -- every relevant facility is within 25 miles of Akwei's address

None of this proves Akwei's claims are true. But the accumulation of verifiable details, independent corroboration, and confirmed classified programs makes dismissal-by-diagnosis ("he was mentally ill") increasingly difficult to sustain as the sole explanation.
