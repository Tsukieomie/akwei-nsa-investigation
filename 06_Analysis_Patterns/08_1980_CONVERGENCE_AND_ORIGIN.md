The school was built specifically for the children of federal employees -- NSA, CIA, DIA, NRO, and defense contractor families. ## 2. The Technology: Byrd's EM Weapons Program

### The Program

- **Name:** Marine Corps Nonlethal Electromagnetic Weapons Project
- **Director:** Dr. Eldon Byrd, Physical Scientist
- **Location:** Naval Surface Weapons Center, White Oak Laboratory, Silver Spring, MD
- **Address:** 10903 New Hampshire Avenue, Silver Spring
- **Distance from 819 Malta Lane:** Approximately 5 miles
- **Duration:** 1980-1983
- **Classification:** Went black after 1983

### What Byrd Demonstrated

1. **Neurochemical release via EM:** 80% increase in opioid peptide release in chick brains using very low frequency electromagnetic radiation
2. **Behavioral control:** Put animals into a stupor by targeting them with specific ELF frequencies
3. **Brain entrainment:** Induced brain waves to synchronize with external EM frequencies
4. **Clandestine applications:** Commissioned in 1981 to develop EM devices for "riot control, clandestine operations, and hostage removal"
5. **Soviet technology evaluation:** Evaluated the LIDA machine, a Soviet EM brain entrainment device

### Byrd's Fate

After the program was classified, Byrd became an advocate for people claiming EM targeting, stating such claims were "seriously worth investigating" -- because he had built the technology himself. Byrd died in 2002.

### Concurrent Program: SLEEPING BEAUTY (DIA)

- **Agency:** Defense Intelligence Agency
- **Period:** 1980s (concurrent with Byrd program)
- **Contractor:** Dr. Michael Persinger
- **Focus:** Classified EM mind-altering weapons research
- **Persinger's 1995 finding:** The energy required for direct brain access is "within the range of contemporary communication networks"

## 3. The Legal Architecture: Executive Order 12333

### The Order

- **Signed:** December 4, 1981
- **By:** President Ronald Reagan
- **Purpose:** Extended powers and responsibilities of US intelligence agencies

### What It Authorized

- Expanded signals intelligence collection by NSA
- Collection of information on US persons under specified circumstances
- "Incidental" collection of domestic communications -- no court authorization required
- Directed federal agency leaders to cooperate fully with CIA requests for information
- Most NSA domestic collection operates under EO 12333, not FISA

### Significance to Akwei

- Signed 5 months after Akwei graduated Northwood HS
- Created the legal framework under which NSA domestic operations could be authorized
- Akwei's complaint specifically references Executive Order 12333, stating his harassment was "not covered by Executive Order 12333" -- meaning he knew the order existed and was arguing his targeting exceeded even its broad authorities
- The Washington Post later described EO 12333 as "the Reagan rule that lets the NSA spy on Americans"

### The NLRB Cover Question

Akwei called Robert a "retired NSA agent." The obituary says NLRB lawyer for 20 years. Three possibilities:

1. **Cover employment:** Intelligence officers routinely use other federal agency positions as cover. NLRB is unremarkable and unquestioned. Obituaries for IC members almost always list cover employer.
2. **Akwei was wrong:** Robert may have been NLRB and nothing more. Akwei may have assumed NSA based on the harassment.
3. **Dual role or detail:** Federal employees can be detailed to other agencies. An NLRB lawyer could serve NSA without public record.

### The Montgomery College to Capitol College Switch

Akwei started at Montgomery College Rockville in September 1982 -- a general community college, though one whose electronics program descends from the Bliss Electrical School (selected by the US Navy as one of six schools for WWII Electronics Training Program, 3,000+ graduates).

After only one year (1982-1983), he transferred to Capitol College. This is a significant move:

- Montgomery College is a general community college with broad curriculum
- Capitol College is a specialized technical school focused on engineering, RF, and telecommunications
- Capitol was founded as a radio engineering correspondence school
- Capitol sits 6 miles from NSA Fort Meade
- Capitol is one of only TWO institutions (the other: Naval Postgraduate School) authorized to offer coursework to NSA personnel through the SEEDS program
- Capitol signed a formal agreement with NSA at the National Cryptologic Museum
- Capitol partnered with the National Cryptologic School (NSA's internal training organization)
- Capitol graduates "hold key cyber positions in the US government, military, and private sector"

The switch from a general community college to the NSA's preferred civilian training institution is either a natural progression for someone interested in electronics -- or evidence of a deeper connection to the intelligence community ecosystem.
