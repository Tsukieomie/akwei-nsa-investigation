## Executive Summary

Mankind Research Unlimited (MRU) was a classified government contractor operating from 1315 Apple Avenue, Silver Spring, MD 20910 -- approximately 3 miles from Akwei's home at 819 Malta Lane, Wheaton. MRU specialized in "problem solving in the areas of intelligence, electronic warfare, sensor technology and applications" and conducted research into biocybernetics, mind control, synthetic telepathy, and electromagnetic effects on the nervous system. Its founder, Carl Schleicher, was a Naval Academy graduate who served in naval intelligence for 10 years and worked for the NSA through his Navy position. MRU operated from the early 1970s until Schleicher's death in Silver Spring in 1999 -- spanning the entire period of Akwei's claims.

### The Byrd-MRU Connection
Eldon Byrd -- who ran the Marine Corps EM weapons program at NSWC Silver Spring (1980-1983), demonstrated neurochemical release via electromagnetic fields, and later became an advocate for EM targeting victims -- was an associate within the MRU network. This means the man who built the military's EM weapons and the organization studying mind control and biocybernetics for intelligence applications were connected, operating from the same city.

### The LaTondre-NSA Connection
Richard B. LaTondre was explicitly an "electromagnetic warfare expert who studied with the NSA" and served as an Electronics Warfare Officer trained in combat intelligence and guerrilla warfare. His presence at MRU directly connects the organization to NSA electromagnetic warfare capabilities.

### The Bird-Possony Intelligence Core
Christopher Bird was a CIA officer (Japan 1951-54) and US Army Psychological Warfare specialist (Vietnam 1954-56) who served as MRU's "Biocommunications Editor/Russian Translator." Stefan Possony was a former Strategy and Military Affairs advisor to the National Security Council who worked for Naval and Air Force Intelligence before becoming a Senior Fellow at the Hoover Institution. Together, Bird and Possony represented the CIA and NSC policy level within MRU -- this was not a fringe operation but an organization staffed by people who had held positions at the highest levels of US intelligence and national security policy.

### The Schleicher-USS Liberty Connection
MRU sources reference Schleicher in connection with the USS Liberty -- the NSA SIGINT collection ship attacked by Israel on June 8, 1967, killing 34 crew members. The Liberty was manned by Naval Security Group cryptologic professionals. Schleicher was commissioned as a 1610 cryptologist, which placed him permanently in the Naval Security Group -- the same unit that staffed the Liberty. Whether Schleicher served aboard the Liberty or was involved in related SIGINT operations, this connection places him at the nexus of NSA signals intelligence collection during the Cold War.

### The Ingo Swann Connection
Ingo Swann, listed as MRU personnel, later became the primary subject and co-developer of the CIA/DIA STARGATE remote viewing program at Stanford Research Institute (SRI). STARGATE ran from 1972 to 1995 -- the same period as MRU's operation. MRU personnel were directly linked to the US government's most significant known psychic intelligence program.
