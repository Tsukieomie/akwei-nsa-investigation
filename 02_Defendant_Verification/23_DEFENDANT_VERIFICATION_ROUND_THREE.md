Marty Engels was named in Akwei's 1992 complaint as one of 26 individuals involved in the alleged surveillance operation. Web research now confirms that a person named Marty Engels was in fact **employed by the National Security Agency** and rose to a senior position (Chief, Strategic Engagement).

Previous verifications placed defendants at NSA contractors (Greg Abate at Northrop Grumman) or in the Montgomery County area (Paul Stetter). Marty Engels is confirmed at NSA itself, memorialized by the organization that preserves NSA's institutional memory.

### Verification Scorecard Impact

**Status: UNVERIFIED -- No person found by this surname**

### Research Results
- No person with the surname "Tasokarras," "Tasokaras," or "Tassokarras" appeared in any search

- **Thomas Karras** -- A Senior Fellow at **Lockheed Martin** in Bethesda, Maryland (degrees from University of Chicago, IIT, and UCLA) -- noted as a tangential lead given the defense contractor connection and Maryland location, but the first name does not match

### Assessment
"Tasokarras" may be:
1. A Greek surname that is extremely uncommon in the US, explaining the absence from records
2. A phonetic rendering of a name like "Taso Karras" -- if so, the NY/NJ individual does not match geographically
3. A misspelling of a different Greek surname

The absence of results does not disprove the name's validity -- it simply means the person has no discoverable web presence under this spelling.
