### Analysis:

**Timeline:** Akwei named Greg Abate in his 1992 complaint. Four years later (1996), Abate begins a 30-year career at Northrop Grumman -- one of the NSA's primary defense contractors.

**Location:** Clarksville, MD is in Howard County, approximately 20 miles from NSA Ft. Meade and approximately 15 miles from the Malta Lane / Kemp Mill area in Silver Spring/Wheaton.

**Employer:** Northrop Grumman's Maryland operations are heavily NSA-facing. The company holds major NSA contracts for cybersecurity, signals intelligence systems, and electronic warfare platforms. Working as a CAD/CAM Engineer at Northrop Grumman for 30 years means designing physical components for defense/intelligence systems.

**The Computershare email:** Computershare is a financial services company that provides employee stock plan administration for large corporations including Northrop Grumman. A @computershare.com email for a Northrop Grumman employee likely relates to employee stock plan access, not a separate job.

**Public records:** A Gregory Abate born April 26, 1964 appears in public records databases associated with Silver Spring, Maryland addresses. If this is the same person, he would have been approximately 28 years old when Akwei filed his complaint in 1992 -- the right age for someone who was a neighbor/acquaintance in the Kemp Mill area.

### Key Question:
Did Greg Abate live in the Kemp Mill / Malta Lane / Silver Spring area in 1980-1992? His later address in Clarksville (~15 miles away) does not confirm proximity during the harassment period, but the Silver Spring public records association suggests he may have been in the area earlier.
