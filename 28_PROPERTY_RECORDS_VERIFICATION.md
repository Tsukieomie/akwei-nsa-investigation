# 28. Property Records Verification: Malta Lane, Silver Spring, MD

> Independent verification of the two Malta Lane addresses named in Akwei v. NSA (Civil Action 92-0449) using Maryland State Department of Assessments and Taxation (SDAT) records, deed references, and public real estate data.

---

## 1. Overview

John St. Clair Akwei's 1992 federal complaint explicitly names two addresses on Malta Lane in Silver Spring, Montgomery County, Maryland:

- **819 Malta Lane** — the Akwei family residence
- **814 Malta Lane** — identified in the complaint as the residence of "RESIDENTS OF 814 MALTA LANE," whom Akwei names as the Kinnecome family

This investigation file documents the verification of both properties using publicly available records.

### Verification Status: **CONFIRMED**

Maryland SDAT records definitively establish that:

1. The Akwei family purchased 819 Malta Lane on November 29, 1977
2. The property was sold by **Claire M. Akwei** on June 29, 2007
3. The family owned the property for **30 continuous years** (1977–2007)
4. This ownership spans the **entire period of alleged harassment** described in the complaint (1980–1992) and extends 15 years beyond the filing date

The seller name "CLAIRE M. AKWEI" in SDAT records independently confirms Akwei family residence at this address — consistent with the complaint's identification of 819 Malta Lane as the plaintiff's home.

Source: [Maryland SDAT](https://sdat.dat.maryland.gov/RealProperty/Pages/default.aspx) — District 13, Account 01347756

- Robert E. "Pete" Kinnecome died on September 12, 1994, at age 56, at his home in Riverdale, MD ([Find a Grave](https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/97704339/robert-e-kinnecome))

- Redfin records show the $116,000 sale on July 30, 1985

### Verification Status: **PARTIALLY CONFIRMED**

The property exists. The sale date, location, and surrounding details are consistent with Akwei's claims. However, definitive confirmation of Kinnecome ownership requires accessing deed /06807/00199 through [MDLandRec.net](https://mdlandrec.net), which requires a free account registration.

### Spatial Relationship

Both properties are on the same street in the Forest Knolls subdivision. They are located across from each other — odd-numbered addresses (819) on one side, even-numbered addresses (814) on the opposite side. This is consistent with Akwei's description of the Kinnecome family as neighbors conducting surveillance from an adjacent property.

### Timeline Consistency

- The Akwei family was at 819 Malta Lane from **1977** onward
- The 1985 sale at 814 Malta Lane is consistent with the Kinnecome family moving in during the period Akwei describes escalating harassment (early-to-mid 1980s)
- Robert Kinnecome's 1994 obituary gives **Riverdale, MD** as his death address — approximately 15 miles south of Malta Lane. This suggests the family may have moved from Silver Spring to Riverdale at some point after the events described in the complaint, or that Kinnecome maintained a separate residence
- The obituary's description of children as "all of Silver Spring" is consistent with the family having established roots in the Silver Spring area during the Malta Lane period
