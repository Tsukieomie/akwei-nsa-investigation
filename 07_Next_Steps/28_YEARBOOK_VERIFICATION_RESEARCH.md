## Assessment

The Ancestry results are significant. Every distinctive target surname from Akwei's 1992 complaint hits in the Northwood High School / Silver Spring yearbook database. This is consistent with the earlier finding that all named defendants appear to be real people from the same neighborhood and school network.

The critical confirmation — which specific year each person appears in — requires either:
1. Ancestry subscription (14-day free trial available)
2. Maryland library card (Montgomery County Public Library provides free Ancestry access)
3. Physical yearbooks via the school or Montgomery County Historical Society
