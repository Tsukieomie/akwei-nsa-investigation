## SECTION E: KEY CORRECTIONS TO INVESTIGATION

Based on the primary court documents, the following corrections are needed:

### 1. Name Correction: "TASO KARRAS" (Two Words)
Previous files used "Tasokarras" as a single surname. The actual court filing clearly shows this is **two separate words: TASO KARRAS**. "Taso" is likely a nickname for Anastasios (Greek).

The background agent in File 23 DID find a Taso Karras (Anastasios T. Karras) in New York/New Jersey. This lead should be re-evaluated now that we know the correct name format.

### 2. Akwei's Northwood Years: 1978-1981
His own statement says he attended Northwood High School from 1978 to 1981. Previous estimates ranged from 1977-1985.

### 3. Akwei's Address: 819 Malta Lane (not 814)
- **Akwei lived at 819 Malta Lane**, Wheaton, MD 20901
- **814 Malta Lane** is across the street -- those residents are also named in the complaint
- Akwei's dossier describes these persons as acquaintances from the neighborhood

### 4. Judge Sporkin's Background
Judge Stanley Sporkin, who dismissed the case in 14 days as "frivolous," served as **CIA General Counsel from 1981-1986** before his appointment to the federal bench. This is verifiable public record.

### 5. Forma Pauperis Application Reveals Economic Devastation
Akwei was earning $90/week at the time of filing. His total income for the prior year was approximately $4,000. He owned nothing. This is consistent with his core claim that NSA personnel systematically prevented him from obtaining employment over a 10-year period.
